Prepare to immerse yourself in the living legacy of authentic Kung Fu, a full-contact martial art system handed down through the generations, directly from the late Grandmaster Yuen Yik Kai. Rooted in the ancient traditions of the Sui Lam Monastery, our 5-animal system stands as a testament to the profound history of martial arts.

At our Exeter club, we embrace enthusiasts of all backgrounds, from beginners setting out on their martial arts journey to seasoned practitioners looking to refine their skills. Our small, tight-knit community values the essence of rigorous training in a friendly and safe environment, delivering tangible results. Here, dedication paves the way to rapid progress, unlocking your true potential, and realizing capabilities you once only read about.

With a history dating back to 1983, the Exeter club has earned a respected reputation within the martial arts world, silently leaving its mark. Join us now to become a part of this enduring legacy in martial arts.
